Getting Started Guide

1. Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is your command center. Here you'll find:

  • Sentiment distribution of your analyzed content
  • Recent analysis results
  • Trend analysis showing sentiment patterns over time

2. Analyzing Content

To analyze new content:

  • Navigate to the analysis section
  • Enter or paste your text content
  • Click "Analyze" to get instant sentiment results
  • View detailed breakdowns including confidence scores and reasoning

3. Understanding Results

Your analysis results include:

  • Overall sentiment (Positive, Negative, or Neutral)
  • Confidence score showing reliability of the analysis
  • Detailed reasoning behind the sentiment classification
  • Visual representations through charts and graphs

4. Premium Features

Premium users get access to:

  • Unlimited sentiment analysis
  • Advanced analytics and trend tracking
  • Detailed sentiment breakdowns
  • Priority support and custom reporting

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?
You can upgrade to Premium from your profile page by clicking the "Upgrade to Premium" button. Premium gives you access to unlimited analysis, advanced features, and priority support.
What features are included in Premium?
Premium includes unlimited sentiment analysis, advanced analytics, detailed sentiment breakdowns, trend tracking, priority support, and custom reporting features.
How accurate is the sentiment analysis?
Our sentiment analysis uses advanced AI models with over 90% accuracy. Each analysis includes a confidence score to indicate the reliability of the result.
Can I export my analysis results?
Yes! You can export your analysis results in both CSV and PDF formats. The exports include sentiment scores, timestamps, and detailed breakdowns of your analyzed content.
How do I interpret the confidence scores?
Confidence scores range from 0-100% and indicate how certain the AI is about its sentiment classification. Higher scores (>80%) indicate very reliable results, while lower scores suggest more ambiguous content.
What types of content can I analyze?
You can analyze any text content including social media posts, customer reviews, comments, and feedback. The system works best with English language content.
How do I track sentiment trends over time?
The dashboard includes interactive charts showing sentiment trends over daily, weekly, and monthly periods. Premium users get access to more detailed trend analysis and custom date ranges.
